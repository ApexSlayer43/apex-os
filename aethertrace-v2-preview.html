How Verification Works

Each evidence item's chain hash is computed as SHA-256(content_hash + ingested_at + previous_hash). The first item uses GENESIS as its previous hash. This creates a tamper-evident chain — altering any single item invalidates all subsequent hashes. AetherTrace cannot modify evidence after ingestion. The recomputed hash above was independently calculated and matches the stored chain hash.
