Append Only

Items are added to the chain. Nothing can be removed. Nothing can be modified. The chain is write-once, read-forever.

Tamper Evident

Each chain hash includes the hash of the previous item. Alter one record and every subsequent link becomes invalid — instantly detectable.

Time-Locked

Timestamps are locked at ingestion. No one — including AetherTrace — can backdate evidence or alter when something was recorded.

AetherTrace Cannot Modify It

The custody is neutral by design. AetherTrace is a trustee, not a party. We cannot alter, delete, or backdate evidence any more than you can.
