How it works
01

Upload your evidence

Photos, inspection reports, receipts, daily logs. Any file. The moment it enters AetherTrace, it gets a SHA-256 hash and an immutable timestamp. Done.

02

The chain seals it

Every item links to the one before it. Change one file and the entire chain breaks visibly. This isn't a promise. It's math.

03

Anyone can verify

Give the attorney the link. They verify the evidence independently. No login. No account. No trust required. The hash proves it.
