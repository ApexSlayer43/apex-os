HELIOS + PRISM — four new marks

01 —— Four Mark Concepts
A
The Trace
Chaos → Sealed → Permanent

A raw evidence signal enters the crystal node and exits sealed. The wave on the left is uncontrolled — the flat line on the right cannot be altered. The diamond node is the moment of sealing. The hash marks are the chain. The name is the mark.

B
The Node
Three Points · One Chain · Hidden A

Three custody nodes connected by precision traces. The hidden form is the letter A — you see it once and you can't unsee it. The apex node glows brightest — the authority point. Lower nodes anchor the chain to earth. Reads as: network, evidence, AetherTrace.

C
The Prism
Crystalline · Refraction · Evidence Within

A cut-crystal form with a custody point at its center. The facets suggest depth, permanence, and mathematical precision — like a diamond that's been forming for millennia. The glow at center says: something is sealed inside. People will stare at this one.

D
The Sigil
A · Arc · Infinite Loop · The Entry Point

Two chain arcs forming the letter A, joined at a glowing apex node — the entry point where evidence enters the chain. The crossbar is the chain connector. The bottom opens downward — the chain continues beyond the mark. Reads as: the letter A, a chain, and an infinite loop simultaneously.

The Glow — Three Intensities

SUBTLE
ACTIVE

Silver doesn't decorate. It confirms. The glow intensity maps to the state of evidence: subtle = idle, active = processing, sealed = confirmed. The mark glows brightest at the moment of custody.

04 —— PRISM's Assessment
MARK A

The Trace

Most conceptually rich. The wave-to-line narrative is perfect. Hard to read at favicon size. Best for hero/hero applications.

MARK B

The Node

Hidden A is a strong idea. Scales perfectly. The apex glow naturally communicates authority. Most versatile of the four.

MARK C

The Prism

Most visually arresting. People will stop at this one. The crystal depth communicates permanence unlike anything else here.

MARK D ★

The Sigil

PRISM's lead. An A that is also a chain that is also an open loop. The apex glow seals it. Works at every size. Has depth without complexity.

The question —

Casey, which mark makes you feel something when you look at it? Not which one is "best" — which one feels like yours? That's the one we develop into a full system. We can also combine elements: Mark A's wave concept in a Mark D framework, or Mark C's crystal as an alternative hero asset.
