PRISM NOTE — The orbital ring is not decorative — it is structural. The ring communicates the core invariant of AetherTrace: a chain of custody that encircles and locks the evidence it contains. The front arc (bright, close) is the seal you can see. The back arc (dim, receding) is the chain you know exists. The traveling node is the live custody event. The word does not sit beside the ring — it is the planet the ring orbits. This mark will not look like any other infrastructure brand because infrastructure brands do not yet think this way. — V3 / ORBITAL SERIES / PRISM APPROVED
