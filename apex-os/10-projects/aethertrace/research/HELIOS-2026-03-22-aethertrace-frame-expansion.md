# HELIOS Vision Analysis — AetherTrace Frame Expansion

## The Reframe
AetherTrace is integrity infrastructure. The same way HTTPS became the invisible layer that made e-commerce possible, AetherTrace is the trust protocol for high-stakes work. Construction is the entry point. The category is verifiable reality. The category doesn't exist yet.

## Fuller System Map
The current system has a structural flaw: the party performing the work controls the evidence of quality. AetherTrace changes the rules — evidence is custodied by a neutral, cryptographic third party.

### Adjacent Systems This Ripples Into
- **Insurance** — Immutable records enable faster claims processing, fraud reduction, and premium adjustment based on AetherTrace usage
- **Bonding** — Verifiable evidence chains change bond risk profiles for contractors
- **Legal** — Court-ready evidence packages become a product for construction litigation firms (channel opportunity)
- **Regulation** — DoD audit deadline Dec 2028 + 70% ESPC savings overstatement = potential mandate trigger for evidence custody on federal contracts

### What Becomes Obsolete
The concept of a documentation dispute. If custody is neutral and cryptographic, arguments shift from "did this happen?" to "what does it mean?"

## SCAMPER Transformations

### Put to Other Uses — Same Structural Flaw in Other Industries
- **Healthcare** — Providers control treatment records. Malpractice disputes face identical evidence custody problems.
- **Environmental compliance** — Companies self-report emissions and remediation. Regulated entity controls the evidence.
- **Real estate inspections** — Inspectors work under conflicting incentive structures.
- **Supply chain** — Factory audits where the factory controls what gets documented.
- The engine is domain-agnostic. The hash chain processes events, not construction photos.

### Eliminate — Radical Simplicity as Moat
Every competitor assumes the user needs project management, scheduling, and communication. AetherTrace eliminates all of it. ONE function: custody. Procore can't copy this by bolting on hashing — their entire architecture assumes they're the project manager. AetherTrace assumes nothing about the project.

### Reverse — Defensive to Trust Signal
Instead of subs uploading to protect themselves, what if primes uploaded to prove they treated subs fairly? What if project owners uploaded to prove proper asset maintenance? "We use AetherTrace" becomes a badge of integrity. Same engine, reversed direction.
