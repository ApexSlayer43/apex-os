Our Belief

Darkness is not the absence of something. It is the presence of everything.

Most people have never seen a truly dark sky. Light pollution has erased it for 80% of the world's population. The Milky Way — the galaxy you live inside — is invisible to most humans alive today.

We exist to reverse that loss. Even if only for one night.

Location

The darkest sky you'll ever stand under

We operate from a private mesa in the Gila Wilderness region of southwestern New Mexico — one of the largest Bortle Class 1 dark sky zones in the continental United States. No city glow for 80 miles in any direction.

The nearest town is Silver City (pop. 9,600). Albuquerque is 3.5 hours. El Paso is 2.5 hours. We provide detailed directions upon booking confirmation.
