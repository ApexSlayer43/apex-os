The Garden

Our treatments begin outdoors

The Kōen garden is a living apothecary. Hinoki cypress, yuzu, shiso, matcha, ginger root, and Japanese mint grow in the same soil where you will walk barefoot before your treatment begins.

The garden is not decoration. It is the first room of every visit. By the time you reach your practitioner, you have already begun to slow down.
