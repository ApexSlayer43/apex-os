Craftsmanship

No power grinders.
No shortcuts.
No exceptions.

Every blade that enters our workshop is sharpened by hand on traditional Japanese whetstones. We use a progressive grit system — starting coarse, finishing mirror-smooth — because that's how steel was meant to be treated.

Whetstones
Naniwa, Shapton, and natural stones sourced from Kyoto. 400 to 8000 grit progression.
Angle Control
Each knife profiled to its intended geometry. Japanese blades at 15°. Western at 20°. Custom on request.
Finishing
Stropped on leather with chromium oxide compound. Edge tested on phone book paper and tomato skin.
