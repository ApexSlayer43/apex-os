What You Need to Know

First time dealing with bail? That's okay.

What does bail cost?
The standard bail bond fee in Tennessee is 10% of the total bail amount. If bail is set at $10,000, the fee is $1,000. We offer payment plans for larger bonds — no credit check required.
What information do I need?
The person's full legal name, date of birth, which jail they're being held in, and the charge if you know it. If you don't have all this, call anyway — we can look it up.
Is this confidential?
Completely. We've been doing this for 20 years. Nothing you tell us is shared with anyone. No judgment. We're here to help, period.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes. We work with your situation. We can discuss options on the phone — we've never turned someone away because of money. We'll find a way to make it work.

Frequently Asked

Common questions

Can I bail someone out if I'm not in Memphis?
Yes. We handle everything remotely. You can call from anywhere, sign paperwork electronically, and pay by phone. You don't need to be physically present.
What if I can't afford the full 10% right now?
Call us. We offer flexible payment plans and will work with your budget. We've been doing this 20 years — we know every situation is different.
How long does it take to get someone out?
Once the bond is posted, release depends on the facility. Shelby County Jail averages 4–8 hours. 201 Poplar can be faster. We'll give you a realistic timeframe on the call.
What happens if they miss a court date?
Call us immediately. Often we can help reschedule without the bond being revoked. The worst thing you can do is nothing — call us and we'll walk you through your options.
